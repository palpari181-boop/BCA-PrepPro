📘 Chapter 1 - Introduction to Computer

Learn the basic concepts of computers, their characteristics, applications, advantages and disadvantages.


💻 What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic device that accepts data as input, processes it according to a set of instructions, stores it, and produces meaningful information as output. It performs calculations and logical operations quickly and accurately.


📊 Data and Information

Data: Raw facts and figures that have no meaning on their own.

Information: Processed and meaningful data that helps in decision-making.


⚙️ Basic Functions of a Computer

  • 📥 Input – Accepts data from the user.
  • ⚙️ Processing – Processes data according to instructions.
  • 💾 Storage – Stores data and information.
  • 📤 Output – Displays the processed result.

⭐ Characteristics of a Computer

  • High Speed
  • High Accuracy
  • Automation
  • Diligence
  • Large Storage Capacity
  • Reliability
  • Versatility

🌍 Applications of Computer

  • 🎓 Education
  • 🏦 Banking
  • 🏥 Healthcare
  • 🏢 Business
  • 📡 Communication
  • 🎮 Entertainment
  • 🔬 Science & Research
  • 🛒 E-Commerce

✅ Advantages of Computer

  • High Speed
  • High Accuracy
  • Large Storage Capacity
  • Automation
  • Saves Time
  • Easy Communication

❌ Disadvantages of Computer

  • No Intelligence
  • Depends on Electricity
  • Cyber Security Risks
  • Health Problems due to long usage

💻 Types of Computers

  • 🖥 Super Computer
  • 🏢 Mainframe Computer
  • 💼 Mini Computer
  • 💻 Micro Computer (Desktop/Laptop)

🖥 Generations of Computer

Generation Technology
First Generation Vacuum Tubes
Second Generation Transistors
Third Generation Integrated Circuits (IC)
Fourth Generation Microprocessors
Fifth Generation Artificial Intelligence (AI)
