📝 Communication Skills - Quick Revision

Revise the complete Communication Skills syllabus in a few minutes.


📘 Revision Points

  • ✔ Communication is the exchange of information.
  • ✔ Effective communication requires a sender, message, medium, and receiver.
  • ✔ Verbal communication includes spoken and written words.
  • ✔ Non-verbal communication includes body language and facial expressions.
  • ✔ Listening is an essential communication skill.
  • ✔ Eye contact improves confidence and engagement.
  • ✔ Group discussions test communication and teamwork.
  • ✔ A resume should be clear, concise, and professional.
  • ✔ Interview success depends on preparation and confidence.
  • ✔ Good communication improves personal and professional relationships.
